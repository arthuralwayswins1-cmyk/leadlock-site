SMS Messaging Consent & Disclosure

How users opt in to receive SMS messages: End users consent to receive SMS messages from LeadLock AI on behalf of participating contractors by submitting a contact form on the contractor's website, texting the contractor's business phone number, or calling the contractor's business and consenting to SMS follow-up. By taking any of these actions, the user agrees to receive conversational SMS messages related to their project inquiry, qualification, and estimate scheduling.

Message content: Messages include project-related follow-up questions, estimate scheduling, appointment confirmations, and reminders. Messages are conversational and specific to the user's inquiry.

Message frequency: Message frequency varies based on the user's inquiry and conversation. Typically 3–10 messages per interaction.

Opt-out: Users can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Upon opting out, no further messages will be sent.

Help: Users can reply HELP at any time to receive support information.

Message and data rates may apply. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Consent to receive messages is not a condition of purchasing any service.
