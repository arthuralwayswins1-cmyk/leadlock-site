The Process

From inquiry to booked estimate — automatically

InfluxLead handles the entire lead response process on behalf of your business, using SMS conversations powered by AI.

01

Lead submits a form

A customer fills out your website contact form and provides their phone number. The form includes explicit SMS consent language before submission.

02

AI responds in seconds

InfluxLead sends a personalized SMS within 60 seconds on your behalf, using a named AI agent. It qualifies the project through natural conversation.

03

Estimate gets booked

When the lead is ready, the AI offers available time slots and books directly into your Google Calendar. You get notified immediately.
